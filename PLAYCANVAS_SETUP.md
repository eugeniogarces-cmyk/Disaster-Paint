# PlayCanvas Setup Guide

## Step 1: Create a PlayCanvas Account
1. Visit [PlayCanvas](https://playcanvas.com).
2. Click on the **Sign Up** button and fill in the required information.

## Step 2: Create a New Project
1. After logging in, go to the dashboard.
2. Click on the **New Project** button.
3. Choose a template or start from scratch.
4. Give your project a name and click **Create**.

## Step 3: Set Up Your Project Environment
1. Familiarize yourself with the PlayCanvas editor interface.
2. Set the project settings by clicking on the gear icon in the top right corner.
3. Configure settings such as project title, description, and version.

## Step 4: Import Assets
1. Click on the **Assets** panel on the left.
2. Use the **Upload** button to import your 3D models, textures, and other assets.
3. Organize your assets into folders for better management.

## Step 5: Create Scenes
1. In the **Scenes** panel, click **New** to create a new scene.
2. Add your imported assets to the scene by dragging them from the Assets panel.
3. Position and rotate your assets using the transform tools.

## Step 6: Add Entities and Components
1. Click on the **Add Entity** button to create a new entity in the scene.
2. Choose the appropriate components (e.g., 3D Model, Light, Camera) from the **Inspector** panel.
3. Customize the properties of each component to suit your needs.

## Step 7: Write Scripts
1. Use the **Script** component to add functionality to your entities.
2. Write scripts in the PlayCanvas scripting API, utilizing JavaScript.
3. Make sure to test your scripts frequently to catch errors early.

## Step 8: Test Your Game
1. Click on the **Play** button in the editor to start the game.
2. Interact with your scene to test all functionalities.
3. Use the console to view any debug information or errors.

## Step 9: Publish Your Project
1. When you are satisfied with your project, click on the **Publish** button.
2. Follow the prompts to make your project live.
3. Share the link with others to showcase your work.

## Conclusion
You have successfully set up your PlayCanvas project! Now you can continue developing your game or application.
